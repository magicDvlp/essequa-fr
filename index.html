Our History

The history of the Maison is born the moment John Sachdeva and Stefano Bonaventura met.

Two very curious and creative minds that cultivate perseverance, constantly in search of pleasure, they refuse to adhere to norms. Together they decided to create olfactory stories that are the result of the impeccabile encounter of the millenary culture of perfumes of the Eastern world and the tradition of Italian and French perfume art. Essequa uses only extremely high quality and highly concentrated components for their fragrances. This gives all Perfumes a much superior persistence than those of others, with the result of making them more layered, more distinguishable and unique with a variety of nuances and notes. Their creations are olfactory, evocative paths full of ancient suggestions, linked to the memories and to the emotions.
All the Perfumes of Maison Essequa contain a minimal part of synthetic essences and ingredients, and this is an act of responsibility towards the Planet and ourselves.
